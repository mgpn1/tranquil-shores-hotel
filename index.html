Welcome to Tranquil Shores

Your Perfect Beachfront Escape

Experience luxury and serenity at our beachfront paradise in Pondicherry.

Nestled along the pristine shores of the Bay of Bengal, Tranquil Shores Hotel & Resort offers an unforgettable blend of coastal charm and modern comfort. Our 25 years of hospitality excellence ensures every guest enjoys personalized service, world-class amenities, and breathtaking ocean views. From our elegant rooms to our award-winning restaurants, every detail is crafted to create your perfect beachside retreat.
